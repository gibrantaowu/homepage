I'm Tao Wu, a first-year PhD student at the College of Computing and Data Science (CCDS), Nanyang Technological University, supervised by Prof. Yewen Pu. I received my bachelor's from Northwestern Polytechnical University and my master's from NTU.

During my master's with Prof. Jianfei Yang, I worked on how noise in human communication affects Embodied Question Answering (EQA), which led to the NoisyEQA and AbstainEQA projects. I am now in the Natural Programming Lab, studying human–agent and human–human communication, with interests in embodied AI and human–robot interaction.
